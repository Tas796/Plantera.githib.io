About Plantera

Plantera is a company working on genetically modifting common crops to increase the effeciency of carbon capturing to sequester as much carbon within the atmosphere to lower carbon levels and concentrationworld wide. Here is how we are planning on acheiving this:

Finding the perfect crop to genetically engineer

Genetically modify chosen crop (potatoes) using CRISPR-Cas9 based gene

Go through an Intial Safety Trials to be approved by th FDA (3-4 year process)

Distributing genetically modified seeds to farmers (initially) to the United States and parts of Europe

Mission

Our goal and mission here at Plantera is to lower the carbon levels in the atmosphere in a cost effective and economically incentivising manner

Problems

Climate Change:

Due to an increase in CO2 levels rising in the atmosphere by the minute, we are getting closer and closer to a point of irreversible damage from natural disasters

Loss of Crops:

Between the years of 2005 and 2015, natural disaster costed the agricultural sector a staggering $96 billion, $29 billion of which was due to droughts around the world

Expensive Capturing Methods:

With an estimated price of $43 to $95 USD per tonne of carbon, conventioanal methods of carbon captur and storage can be expensive, losing any kind of incetive for large companies to adapt this method

Solutions

Augmenting Root Size:

Modification of the EXO70A3, TOB1, and NAC coding genes to augment the root, dirrectly correalting to the plants' drought resistancy

Increasing Photosynthesis Efficiency:

Modification of chemical pathways to increase the the rate and efficiency of carbon capture

Increasing Decomposition Resistance

Altercation of chemical composition to increase the length of time the carbon is fixated
